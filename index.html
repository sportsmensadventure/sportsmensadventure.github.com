About

The South Story Sportsmen's Adventure is being hosted by Faith Baptist Church of Cambridge. It will be held on Saturday, February 4, 2012, at the Ballard East Elementary School just outside Cambridge, Iowa. This event is absolutely free and open to whoever is interested. For more information, call Jacob at (515)-822-3626.

Schedule for 2012

The seminars include:

  • Home Game Processing
  • Introduction to Pistol Shooting
  • Trapping
  • And more!
The seminars begins at 10:00 AM, and Hickory Park's Barbecue will be served for lunch at noon. A hunter's safety course is also being held from 9:00 AM to 12:00 AM. There will be doorprizes.
