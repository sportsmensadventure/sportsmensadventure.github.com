About

    The South Story Sportsmen's Adventure is being hosted by Faith Baptist Church of Cambridge. It will be held on Saturday, February 4, 2012, at the Ballard East Elementary School just outside Cambridge, Iowa. This event is absolutely free and open to whoever is interested. For more information, call Jacob at (515)-822-3626.

Schedule for 2012

    10 A.M.

  • Game Processing by Tim Hudson of Golly's Locker in Maxwell, IA
  • Introduction to Pistol Shooting by Jim Egeland, Reserve Story County Deputy of Madrid, IA
  • Introduction to Muzzleloading by Jacob Saylor of Cambridge, IA

    • 11 A.M.

  • Game Processing by Tim Hudson
  • Mink and Raccoon Trapping by Ron Fleenor of Cambridge IA
  • Introduction to Pistol Shooting by Jim Egeland

    • Noon

  • Hickory Park's Barbecue served for lunch

    • 12:45 A.M.

  • Keynote by Tim Miller

A hunter's safety course is also being held from 9:00 AM to 12:00 AM. There will be doorprizes.
